About Me

I am a PhD Candidate in Chemical Engineering and MS in Computer Science at the University of Minnesota (Twin Cities).

My work sits at the intersection of Molecular Simulations and Machine Learning. I specialize in developing data-driven workflows and Python tools to accelerate the discovery of nanoporous materials for sustainable industrial applications, such as natural gas storage and ammonia separation.

With a background spanning across IIT Kanpur and BIT Mesra, I am passionate about bridging the gap between fundamental chemical engineering and modern computational science to solve high-impact environmental challenges.
